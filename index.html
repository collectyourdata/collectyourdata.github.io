What is Collect Your Data?

Collect Your Data is a place where you can work with others, or use what others have created, to gather information from devices and then have the data posted into analytical software to investigate and analyze (and play with). This first started out by me wanting to capture the GPS coordinates on my iPhone to then analyze and play with. I could view my location with Google and Apple iPhone Apps but I wanted to do more with the data and also have the data available to use for whatever else I thought of in the future.
